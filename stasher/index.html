how stasher works.

Track My Cash

Kids can track their cash from anywhere on the planet using Stasher, available free on the Apple App Store.

Birthdays, holidays, or just for kicks, Stasher allows parents and relatives to easily and safely contribute to a child’s saving account from their mobile devices absolutely free.

Complete My Missions to Get Paid

Kids complete Missions set up by their Parent’s to earn rewards. A Mission is a task, chore, or goal. This can be taking out the trash, or getting a good grade on that Math test.

The best parts about missions are the rewards: from real cold hard cash deposited straight into kid’s savings accounts!

Keep My Money Safe

Kids can keep their money safely in their savings accounts, and away from siblings, parents, and even bad guys! Stasher focuses on teaching kids to save money and watch their balance explode.
  • Track your money from any device on-the-go
  • Earn and save real money, yes, REAL mullah, cash, money!
  • Create and track Missions, like chores and goals, to earn rewards
  • Get rewarded with money, badges, and more! How awesome is that!!
