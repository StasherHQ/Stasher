About Stasher

STASHER was founded in 2013 with one mission: to put the power of saving in the hands of kids. Available for free through the Apple App Store, Stasher allows kids to earn real money, get paid, and watch their savings explode over time. At Stasher, we’re transforming that traditional old piggy bank into the ultimate stashing app that kids deserve today.

STASHER is dedicated to getting as much money stashed into each kid’s savings account as possible. We’re focused on forming new habits early. And it’s never too early to start saving. Stasher wants to focus on saving in a way that’s fun and educational for everyone. It’s almost like we’re teaching kids to eat their vegetables, except without the broccoli.

THE President said it himself: it should be the nation’s goal to have every child born with a savings account, from Day 1. Stasher too has the same ambition, and we are working hard to recruit the next generation of savers.
