Knox Payments

Stasher’s number one priority is money safety. That’s why we chose to partner with Knox Payments to facilitate all money transfers using Stasher. With Knox, we want to keep your banking information totally secure and protected.

Knox only stores the minimum information necessary to make a transaction on your behalf. That way, your sensitive information is never stored.

Knox employs 128-bit TLS encryption and the same physical security standards as your bank, keeping your information safe as you move it with Stasher.

To learn more about Knox Payments, click here.

Money Movements

Stasher permits Parents to move money using ACH payments, which transfer funds directly between two bank accounts. These payments are completed through an electronic network called the Automated Clearing House (ACH), which is regulated by NACHA and the Federal Reserve.

Children never have direct access to their bank accounts, the ability to transfer money, or the option to view any banking information.

Privacy Policy

Stasher is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our site and mobile application. Stasher’s Children’s Online Privacy Policy explains our information collection, disclosure, and parental consent practices with respect to information provided by children under the age of 13.
